Daal, the way Maa made it

  • Lentils (yellow)1 cup
  • Water3 cups
  • Turmerica pinch
  • Saltto taste
  • Ghee1 spoon
  • Cumin seedssome
Wash the lentils until the water runs clear. Do not rush this. "This is how you learn patience. You cannot hurry clarity."
Boil with water, turmeric, and salt. Watch the pot. It boils over when you turn your back. "Things fall apart the moment you stop paying attention. Stay present."
In a separate pan, heat the ghee. It should be hot but not smoking. "Passion without discipline is just fire. It burns the food."
Add the cumin seeds. Wait for them to crackle. You have to listen for it. "Listen to what is happening, not what you expect to happen."
Pour the temper over the lentils. It will hiss loudly. Cover it immediately to trap the smoke. "Some pain is loud. Contain it. Let it flavor the rest of your life."
Serve warm. "I love you. I am feeding you. Survive."
