TERMS & CONDITIONS OF HUMAN EXISTENCE

UNIVERSAL CONTRACT · VERSION 1.0 (FINAL) · NON-NEGOTIABLE
EFFECTIVE DATE: YOUR BIRTH DATE
BY EXISTING, YOU AGREE TO ALL TERMS BELOW.
BINDING
CLAUSE 1 — CONSCIOUSNESS
1.1 THE PARTY OF THE FIRST PART (hereinafter 'YOU') is hereby granted awareness of its own existence.
NOTE: This cannot be revoked or paused. Not even during meetings.
You will know you exist. This is occasionally wonderful and occasionally the problem.
CLAUSE 2 — LOVE
2.1 YOU are permitted and in fact required to experience attachment to other instances of the human experience.
2.2 Said attachment carries risk of loss. Risk is non-negotiable.
See Clause 7 (Grief). These cannot be uncoupled.
You will love people. Some will leave. This is the price of Clause 2.1 and worth it.
CLAUSE 3 — TIME
3.1 THE PARTY is granted a single non-renewable allocation of time.
3.2 Duration: variable and undisclosed at contract signing.
3.3 Unused time does not roll over.
Attempts to 'save time' are noted and generally unsuccessful.
You get one pass. The length is not told to you. Please use it.
CLAUSE 4 — FAILURE
4.1 Periodic failures are required components of the human experience.
4.2 Failure at a thing does not constitute failure as a person.
This distinction is often missed. See also: Clause 4.2.
You will fail things. This is scheduled. It is how Clause 11 (Growth) occurs.
CLAUSE 5 — THE BODY
5.1 YOU are issued a biological unit for the duration of the contract.
5.2 The unit requires maintenance and will deteriorate regardless.
5.3 The unit is not YOU. It is your vehicle.
The unit will sometimes be the wrong size, the wrong shape, or not work correctly. This was not in the original spec.
CLAUSE 6 — CHANGE
6.1 YOU are contractually required to be different at age 40 than at age 20.
6.2 This is not failure. This is compliance.
The person you used to be was correct for then. The person you're becoming is correct for now. Both are you.
CLAUSE 7 — GRIEF
7.1 Loss is included in the human experience package.
7.2 See Clause 2 (Love). These cannot be separated.
"7.3 The size of grief is proportional to the size of what was valued."
7.4 This is not punishment. This is math.
Grief is love with nowhere to go. It means the love was real.
CLAUSE 8 — JOY
8.1 JOY is available and recurrent throughout the contract period.
8.2 Joy is often found in places not included in the original plan.
8.3 The user is encouraged to notice Joy when it occurs, as the human unit is prone to overlooking it.
Joy does not require announcement or permission.
CLAUSE 9 — PURPOSE
9.1 Purpose is not pre-installed.
9.2 Purpose is assembled through use.
"9.3 The user should not wait for purpose to arrive before beginning."
Begin. Purpose follows use. Not the other way.
CLAUSE 10 — TERMINATION
10.1 This contract terminates.
10.2 Date is not disclosed at signing.
10.3 Terms are non-negotiable.
"10.4 All that was done, said, and felt during the contract period: permanent."
10.5 Love: permanent.
10.6 This is the point.
BY EXISTING, YOU HAVE ACCEPTED THESE TERMS.
