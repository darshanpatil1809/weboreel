A Good Life
Yields: 1 Human Experience
INGREDIENTS
Meaningful Work 1/3 portion
Does not mean 'dream job'. Means effort directed at something that matters to someone, even if that someone is just you.
Uninterrupted Rest 8 hours, daily
Crucial binding agent. If omitted, the entire structure becomes brittle and collapses under pressure.
Deep Connection 2-3 handfuls
Quality over quantity. Surface-level networking cannot be substituted. Must be raw, uncurated, and face-to-face when possible.
Solitude to taste
Not to be confused with isolation. Solitude is a choice. Required to digest the other ingredients properly.
Novelty & Play 1 dash
Doing something you are terrible at simply because it is fun. Prevents the mixture from becoming stale.
PREPARATION
Combine ingredients slowly. Do not rush.
Note: Measurements are approximate. Adjust based on season of life.
