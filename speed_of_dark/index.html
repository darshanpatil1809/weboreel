The speed of light is 299,792,458 m/s.
It is the absolute speed limit of the universe. Nothing can travel faster.
Light requires energy. It is a photon traveling through space. It is a physical thing taking a physical journey.
But what is the speed of dark?
Darkness is not a particle. Darkness has no mass, no energy, no physics to obey.
Darkness is simply the absence of light.
Therefore, darkness does not travel. It is already there. It is waiting.
When light leaves, darkness fills the void instantly. At infinite speed.
The speed of dark is faster than the speed of light.
A reminder that presence requires effort.
Absence is the default state of the universe.
