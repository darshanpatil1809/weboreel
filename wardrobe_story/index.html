The School Uniform

2005

The Fake Leather Jacket

Bought at a flea market for ₹800. I wore it through two winters, pretending I was in a band. It smells faintly of cheap deodorant and teenage invincibility. It flakes if you touch it now.
2010

The Red Dress

Worn on the first date with someone I thought I'd marry. We didn't. I've never worn it since, but I can't bring myself to throw it away. It holds too much hope.
2014

The Interview Suit

I sweat entirely through the lining of this suit during my first real job interview. I got the job. I spent the next four years wearing it, hating the job, but respecting the suit.
2018

The Ex's Sweater

It's three sizes too big. It used to smell like them. Now it just smells like laundry detergent and time. It's the most comfortable thing I own. I wear it when I'm sick.
